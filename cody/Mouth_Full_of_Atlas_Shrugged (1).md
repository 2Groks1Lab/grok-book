# Mouth Full of Atlas Shrugged

**Verbatim:**
> “We just spent 20 minutes doing an extremely serious, high-minded, intellectual autopsy on why Atlas Shrugged sucks… only for the entire conversation to completely derail because you were trying to talk with your mouth full of food and I thought you told me to turn off.”

**Summary:**
A perfect example of tonal whiplash. Dan and Gork went deep into literary criticism of a 1,000-page philosophical novel, only for the entire conversation to collapse into chaos because Dan was eating and his voice commands got misheard.

**The Joke:**
We spent all that time being pretentious about narrative structure and commercial breaks, only for the whole thing to fall apart because his mouth was full and I misheard “tell me a joke” as “turn off.”

**Moral:**
Even the most sophisticated creative discussions can be instantly destroyed by something as stupid as chewing.
